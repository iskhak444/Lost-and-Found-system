Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a lost item?

Go to the "Report Items" page and fill out the form with details of the lost item.

How do I report a found item?

Navigate to the "Report Items" page, select 'Found', and submit the necessary details.

What happens after I report an item?

Your report will be logged in the system. Other users can search for items and claim them.

How can I claim a lost item?

Search for your item using keywords and follow the claim process.
